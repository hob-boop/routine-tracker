Personal Coaching Brand

  • I made this mistake for 2 years before I fixed it.
  • Most barbershops fail because of this one thing.
  • Here's what I track every week that changed everything.
  • Running a team of contractors taught me this.
  • The business advice no one gave me when I opened.

Client Acquisition

  • Not sure what to ask for? Start with this.
  • This is what a $[X] cut looks like versus a cheap one.
  • Wednesday slots are open. Here's why that's rare.
  • We've got 3 spots left this Saturday. Serious inquiries only.

  • To our regulars — this one's for you.
  • Your hair grows whether you rebook or not. Book first.
  • If your cut is overdue, this is your sign.
  • The clients who come every 3 weeks look the sharpest.
  • How long has it been? We're still here.
